Welcome to the Atlanta Art Museum

The Atlanta Art Musuem is located in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia. This art musuem is one of the most famous musuems in the country, and boasts a mix of both modern and traditional art pieces. This makes it one of the best tourist attractions in all of Georgia, as the Atlanta Art Musuem averages around 1,000 guests per day. Our art musuem not only displays the beautiful art of famous artists from around the world, but also recognizes minority art and the art of aspiring artists.
